Spaghetti

Description

This is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring a rich tomato sauce, tender spaghetti noodles, and optional meat toppings. Perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (optional)
  • Garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the spaghetti noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. If using meat, add it to the pan and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the pan, season with salt and pepper, and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Mix the cooked spaghetti with the sauce or serve the sauce on top of the noodles.
  6. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil, if desired.
