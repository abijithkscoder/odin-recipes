Tacos

Description

Tacos are a versatile and delicious Mexican dish made with a variety of fillings like seasoned meat, fresh veggies, and flavorful toppings, all wrapped in a soft or crispy tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Soft or hard taco shells
  • Ground beef, chicken, or beans (for a vegetarian option)
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded lettuce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef or chicken in a skillet over medium heat, adding taco seasoning according to package instructions.
  2. Warm the taco shells in the oven or microwave.
  3. Prepare toppings by shredding lettuce, dicing tomatoes, and grating cheese.
  4. Assemble the tacos: add the meat or beans as the base, then layer with lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream, salsa, and guacamole, if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
