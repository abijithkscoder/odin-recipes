Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious and classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, and rich meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  3. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes.
